GreenBowl — From the pantry to delicious meals

"GreenBowl — a project from us Pinky Bisexuals — helps turn your fridge into an inspiration for cooking. Type in what you have, and we will suggest Vietnamese vegan recipes: diverse, environmentally-friendly, and easy to make at home. No need for professional chefs — a heart filled with passion for cooking is enough."

Our Missions

  • Assist homecooks
  • Encourage home cooking, reducing food waste
  • Grow a community sharing creative vegan meals

Values

  • Approachable: accessible to all cooks
  • Fun: free to add your twists, with joy
  • Eco-friendly: encourage utilizing and minimizing scrap
  • Homemade: prioritize fresh ingredients, simple recipes

Behind GreenBowl

A small team of homecooks, content volunteers, and Pinky Bisexuals — all responsible for developing easy-to-make Vietnamese vegan recipes.
